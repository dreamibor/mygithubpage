How do I get a DICE account?
Getting help The School's Computing Staff provide many of the computing services, known as DICE services, used within the School of Informatics. The front line support team provides user support for these facilities. Please use the support form to contact us.
Other computing services are provided by the University's Information Services (IS). You can contact the IS Helpline via UniDesk.
You can also ask for help at InfBase which provides a range of advice, including programming tips. Further information and session times are available on the InfBase website.
